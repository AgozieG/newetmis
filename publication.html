Publication & Indexing

Springer Publication

The conference proceedings will be published in Springer series STEAM-H: Science, Technology, Engineering, Agriculture, Mathematics & Health - indexed by Scopus, Mathematical Reviews, and zbMATH.

STEAM-H is a prestigious series that publishes high-quality proceedings from international conferences. The series covers a broad range of topics in science, technology, engineering, agriculture, mathematics, and health.

Indexing

  • Scopus - The largest abstract and citation database of peer-reviewed literature
  • Mathematical Reviews - Comprehensive coverage of mathematical research
  • zbMATH - The most comprehensive abstracting and reviewing service in pure and applied mathematics

EDP Sciences Publication

The conference proceedings will also be published in EDP Science ITM Web of Conferences series - indexed by Conference Proceedings Citation Index (Web of Science), DOAJ, IET INSPEC and Technology Collection (ProQuest).

Indexing

  • Conference Proceedings Citation Index (Web of Science) - Part of Thomson Reuters Web of Science
  • DOAJ - Directory of Open Access Journals
  • IET INSPEC - The leading bibliographic database providing abstracts and indexing to the world's scientific and technical papers
  • Technology Collection (ProQuest) - Comprehensive coverage of advanced technology literature

Publication Process

  1. Paper Submission - Authors submit papers through Microsoft CMT
  2. Peer Review - Double-blind review process by expert reviewers
  3. Notification - Authors receive acceptance/rejection notification
  4. Camera-Ready Submission - Authors submit final version of accepted papers
  5. Copyright Transfer - Authors transfer copyright to the publisher
  6. Publication - Papers are published in the conference proceedings

Important Notes

  • All papers must be presented at the conference to be included in the proceedings
  • At least one author must register for the conference
  • The publication partner reserves the right to exclude any paper that does not meet their quality standards
  • Authors must strictly follow the formatting guidelines
