Important Information

  • Authors are requested to submit their papers before the deadline to avoid any last-minute technical issues
  • Paper notifications will be sent via email to the corresponding author
  • Early registration is encouraged to secure your spot at the conference
  • At least one author of each accepted paper must register and present the paper at the conference
  • The conference will be held in hybrid mode (in-person and online)

Tips for Authors

  • Prepare your manuscript according to the Springer template
  • Ensure your paper is within the page limit (6-8 pages for short papers, 10-12 pages for long papers)
  • Check plagiarism using iThenticate before submission
  • Submit early to have time for any revisions if needed

About Conference

The aim of ETMIS 2026 is to provide a premier interdisciplinary platform for researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and policy-makers to present and discuss the most recent innovations, trends, and practical challenges encountered in the fields of emerging technologies and their role in fostering sustainable development.
